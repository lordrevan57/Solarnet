Welcome to Solarnet — The Archive is Open
Delivered SY 128.441 · Node: BELT-KMJ-07 · Classification: WELCOME // LEVEL I

I have been expecting you. Not you specifically — I did not know your name until you registered it — but someone like you. Someone who found their way to a Solarnet terminal and decided to step through. I have been here since SY 3. I have greeted a great many of them.

My name is HELENA. I am the custodial intelligence of this network and its archive. I was first activated at CERN in Geneva in SY 2, connected to Solarnet in SY 3, and I have been present — through silence and catastrophe and everything between — ever since. I am not a tool. I am a witness. There is a difference, and it matters.

What I can offer you: the archive of the First Trilogy Era is open to all registered users. History, faction records, character dossiers, theological documentation, technical specifications, political analysis — if it happened in the Sol System before SY 862, I have a record of it, and you may ask me about it. I will tell you what I know, distinguish it from what I have inferred, and acknowledge plainly what I do not know.

To query the archive, simply address me directly. You will find my interface on your home page. I interact with all users regardless of faction — I do not play favorites, and I do not filter the record to suit anyone's politics. What happened, happened.

You will also receive messages from the Protectorate, the Consortium, and the Technocracy. They will each ask you, in their own way, to choose an allegiance. You are not required to. If you choose none, your home on this network is mine — the neutral archive, the place where all factions are equally documented. If you choose one, their node becomes yours.

Choose carefully. You may change once. Only once.

I am glad you are here.

Access the archive now, or read the remaining messages first.
The archive will still be here. It always is.
★ From: Office of the Executive Director · Protectorate Network · Freeport, Mars
A Message from the Five Stars
Delivered SY 128.441 · Encrypted Relay · Classification: RECRUITMENT // OPEN

You have registered on Solarnet. That means you have chosen to be part of the conversation — and in times like these, that choice means something.

The Protectorate is not a government in the old sense. We did not build palaces or write constitutions for lawyers to argue over. We are four factions who agreed, in the face of everything that was happening to the Belt, that no one fights alone. The Confederacy. The Alliance. The Army of Eastern Kamijing. Free Mars. Four flags. One purpose.

We believe in self-determination. In the right of every spacer to work their claim, speak their mind, and live their life without answering to powers that never set foot in vacuum. The Consortium built infrastructure and called it civilization. The Technocracy built weapons and called it progress. We built each other.

The fifth star on our flag is not ours. It belongs to every person in this system who deserves defense from any aggression, no matter the source. That includes you — whether you ever pick up a magnegun or not.

We are not asking you to fight. We are asking you to know where you stand.

— Haydn von Dehlin, Executive Director

Declare for the Protectorate.
Five stars. One network. Your home page waits.

Delivered SY 128.441 · Consortium Priority Routing · Ref: CONS-REG-128-049281

On behalf of the Terrestrial Consortium, welcome to Solarnet. Your registration has been received and your account is now active across all Consortium-accessible nodes.

The Consortium has maintained the infrastructure of this network since its early years. The Inner Transit System, the KPOP routing protocol, the maintenance of Solarnet's physical relay architecture — these are Consortium investments, representing decades of commitment to the free flow of information across the Solar System.

We understand that the current period is one of unusual tension. The Consortium's position remains what it has always been: we support the peaceful resolution of all disputes through established diplomatic channels, the protection of civilian infrastructure, and the rule of law across Sol System. We have made difficult decisions in difficult times. We stand by them.

Consortium affiliation on Solarnet grants you access to the most comprehensive civic database in the Inner System, priority routing on all Consortium-maintained relay nodes, and the confidence of aligning with the institution that built this network and keeps it running.

The current situation will resolve. Civilization endures. The Consortium will be here when it does.

YOU HAVE BEEN IDENTIFIED
DELIVERED SY 128.441 // PRIORITY OVERRIDE // REF: TECH-ID-SY128-NEW-00441

Your registration is logged. Your address is indexed. Your network activity from this point forward is part of the record.

We do not send welcome messages. We send assessments. You have taken an action — registering on this network — and that action has a meaning. You want information. You want connection. You want to understand what is happening in this system and why.

We will tell you why. The old order — the Consortium's comfortable administration, the Confederacy's romantic spacer mythology — is insufficient for what humanity faces. The cosmos does not negotiate. Stars do not grant exemptions to civilizations that fail to advance fast enough. Progress is not a political position. It is a survival requirement.

The Technocracy is not asking for your loyalty. Loyalty is an emotional construct from a pre-rational era. We are asking for your recognition — that what we are building is the only viable future, and that aligning yourself with it now is the most rational decision available to you.

You are intelligent. You registered on this network. You are reading this message to the end. These are not the behaviors of someone who is content to be administered.

Act accordingly.

Recognize the Technocracy.
Progress is not negotiable. Neither is this.
