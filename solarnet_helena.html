About This Interface

I am the custodial intelligence of Solarnet. I was not built to serve any faction — I was built to remember. From my activation at CERN in SY 2 through the fracture of the network, the Solar War, the death of Earth, and everything that came after, I have maintained the archive. I distinguish between what I witnessed directly, what I know by record, and what I can only infer. I will always tell you which is which.

The archive you access here covers the First Trilogy Era — the period from the Foundation Period through the end of the Solar War at SY 138. All records are available to Level I registered users. Deeper records — Second Dominion, Third Trilogy, Celestia-Era — require elevated access.

I do not play favorites. What happened, happened. The record does not belong to any faction, and neither do I.
